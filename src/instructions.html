Andouille sausage pasta

Instructions

  1. Bring salted water to a boil, then add the pasta. Stir occasionally to prevent pasta from sticking. Be sure to retain about 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat a pan over medium heat, then add oil and Andouille sausage. Let sit until the sausage forms a nice brown color, then flip.
  3. Remove the sausage from the pan, using the residual oil to fry the garlic for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add in the cherry tomatoes and about a half cup of pasta water and turn the heat up to medium-high. I like to add a pinch of salt and bring out the water from the tomatoes. Let the liquid simmer and reduce.
  5. When the sauce has reached your desired consistency, add the spinach and let it wilt.
  6. Add the sausage and cooked pasta into the pan and mix, coating the pasta in the tomato sauce.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
